About ThreatAware

ThreatAware is an interactive cybersecurity education platform designed to teach you how to protect yourself in the digital world.

Why I Built This:

The digital landscape has never been more dangerous. With new threats emerging every day - from sophisticated phishing schemes to AI-powered scams - it's becoming increasingly difficult for everyday people to stay safe online. I created this project because I believe that if I can help even one person recognize a threat before it's too late, it's worth it.

Sometimes the best way to remember something is to teach it. By building this platform, I'm not just helping others learn - I'm reinforcing these critical safety habits for myself too. Because in cybersecurity, staying vigilant isn't a one-time thing; it's an ongoing practice.

What You'll Learn:

  • Common Attack Types - Understand phishing, malware, social engineering, and more
  • Defense Strategies - Learn practical steps to protect your data
  • Security Best Practices - Build habits that keep you safe online
  • Real-World Scenarios - Practice identifying threats in interactive simulations

Why This Matters:

Cybercrime costs the world trillions of dollars annually, and most attacks target individuals, not corporations. By understanding how hackers think, you become a much harder target.

Stay safe. Stay informed. Stay secure.
