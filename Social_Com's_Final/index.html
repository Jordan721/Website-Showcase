About Social Com's

Social Com's is a revolutionary social media platform designed to prioritize user privacy and authentic connections. Founded by Jeff Davis in 2018 in response to major data privacy scandals, our mission is to create a trustworthy alternative to traditional social networks.

Based in Downtown Brooklyn, we're committed to transparency, ethical data practices, and building a community where users feel safe and valued.

About This Project

What started as a class project has evolved into something much cooler! I created this social media mockup to explore what privacy-first networking could look like, wrapped in sleek, modern design.


Think of this as the "2.0" version - I've completely rebuilt it with modern web techniques and fresh ideas. Curious about where it all began? Check out the wireframes to peek at the original beta concept. It's like seeing the blueprint before the mansion was built!


From responsive layouts that look great on any device to smooth interactions and multiple pages (account settings, feeds, moderator tools), this project showcases what happens when you combine creativity with code.
